This site gives you a quick overview of how the last 10 seasons of the NBA was played out.

You can find all the champions and the runner-ups for the past 10 seasons in the "Years" page. All the regular season MVPs and finals MVPs are listed in the "Players" page.

These players' current and past teams are listed in the "Teams" page.
